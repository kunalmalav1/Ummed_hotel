A Legacy of
Rajputana Grandeur

Nestled in the heart of Jaipur, Ummed Hotel is a testament to Rajputana grandeur reimagined for the modern connoisseur. Every corner tells a story of artistry, warmth, and timeless refinement — where centuries of royal hospitality meet contemporary luxury.

From our celebrated Sweet Counter to our world-class culinary destinations, every experience at Ummed is crafted to leave an indelible mark on the soul.
